## Install on iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1. **Open in Safari**
   - Navigate to your app URL in Safari browser
   - Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up)
   
2. **Add to Home Screen**
   - Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
   - Edit the name if desired (default: "Parlay Pro")
   - Tap "Add" in the top right

3. **Launch the App**
   - Find the Parlay Pro icon on your home screen
   - Tap to launch in full-screen mode
   - Enjoy native-like experience!

## Install on Android

1. **Open in Chrome**
   - Navigate to your app URL in Chrome browser
   - Tap the menu (three dots) in the top right
   
2. **Install App**
   - Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
   - Confirm installation
   
3. **Launch the App**
   - Find Parlay Pro in your app drawer
   - Tap to launch
   - Works offline with cached data!

## Troubleshooting

**App won't install?**
- Make sure you're using Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
- Check you have storage space available
- Try clearing browser cache and retry

**Offline mode not working?**
- Visit the app while online first to cache data
- Service worker needs initial network connection
- Check browser settings allow service workers

**Updates not showing?**
- Close and reopen the app completely
- Clear app data and reinstall
- Service worker updates automatically on app reopen

## Support

- You're on the latest version (v2.0)
